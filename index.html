    Hey there, I'm Grace!

    I'm an actor, musician, and dancer living in Chicago.

    Headshot Resume Instagram Youtube

    I recently graduated from Northwestern University with a degree in theater and music. I am proudly represented by Shirley Hamilton Talent.

    At Northwestern, I specialized in studies of ensemble-based works, both theatrically and musically. I now consider collaboration to be the core of my main artistic interests. I have been proud to call the Midwest home for 22 years, as it contains most of my favorite foods (Chicago’s deep-dish pizza), landmarks (Milwaukee’s Pabst Theater), and people (too many family members to name).

    I began 2020 at Northbrook Theater, playing the Mom in “Knuffle Bunny”, directed by Randy White. I’m thrilled to rejoin the same cast for a intended remounted production at the Greenhouse Theater in June.

    Like many other artists, several of my upcoming projects have been put on hold or cancelled due to COVID-19 restrictions. You can stay up to date with my current at-home artistic endeavors by checking out my Instagram and Youtube pages, or exploring the work of my band, The Fisher Cats. We released our first EP earlier this year on Spotify, Apple Music, and Amazon Music. Give us a listen here! Thanks for saying hi and stay healthy.
