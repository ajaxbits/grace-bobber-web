As a music director for my collegiate a cappella group…

I led rehearsal warm-ups, created and taught arrangements for the co-ed group of 20, implemented a rehearsal calendar, and coordinated gigs on Northwestern's campus and in the greater Chicago area. During my time as a music director, the group reached its highest number of views on any of our Youtube videos. Currently, our arrangement of "River" by Bishop Briggs sits at over 7 million views.

I also was one of the group’s most frequent arrangers, using Sibelius and Finale software to create 8-part vocal arrangements. Below, you can find some of my favorite projects that I either led or collaborated on. Check out more information on THUNK here.
